## Who I Am

7+ years building cloud infrastructure that doesn't wake engineers up at 3am.

I'm currently a **Senior Cloud & DevOps Engineer at Publicis Sapient**, where I design and run the platform infrastructure for enterprise clients — AKS clusters, GitOps pipelines, Terraform modules, DevSecOps toolchains, and GenAI/LLMOps foundations on Azure.

Before that, 5+ years at **TCS Digitate** automating infrastructure, containerising legacy systems, and making deployments boring (in the best way).

What I actually care about:
- **Automation first** — if a human is doing it twice, it should be a script
- **Security baked in** — not bolted on after the fact
- **Platforms, not snowflakes** — reusable, self-service infra that scales across teams
- **Observable systems** — you can't fix what you can't see

## How I Think About This Work

Most infrastructure problems are really **people problems in disguise** — teams that can't deploy because the platform isn't self-service, engineers on-call for things that should be automated, security reviewed at the end instead of the beginning.

I've worked at a product company (TCS Digitate) and a global consultancy (Publicis Sapient). Both taught me the same thing: **the best infrastructure is the kind nobody notices** — it just works, scales, and stays secure while the product team ships.
